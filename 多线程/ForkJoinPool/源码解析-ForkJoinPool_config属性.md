**1.ForkJoinPool构造函数**

**parallelism使用1-16位表示**
**mode使用17-32位表示**

```java

this.config = (parallelism & SMASK) | mode;
↓
↓
//parallelism默认等于availableProcessors-1
//我的电脑是8核,所以parallelism等于7
//"& SMASK"是保证不能大于SMASK
parallelism & SMASK
  0000000000000000 0000000000000111
  0000000000000000 1111111111111111
//mode默认等于LIFO_QUEUE
  0000000000000000 0000000000000111
  0000000000000000 0000000000000000
↓
↓
0000000000000000 0000000000000111

//如何通过config获取mode
config & MODE_MASK
  0000000000000000 0000000000000111
  1111111111111111 0000000000000000
↓
↓
//等于LIFO_QUEUE
0000000000000000 0000000000000000
```
